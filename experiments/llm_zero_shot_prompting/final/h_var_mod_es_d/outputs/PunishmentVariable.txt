When comparing studies where Sequential punishment is Simultaneous and studies where Sequential punishment is Sequential leadership-by-example, cooperation from studies involving Leader as Punishment agent is significantly higher than cooperation from studies involving based on Network as Punishment agent.
When comparing studies where Punishment agent is Leader and studies where Punishment agent is Network, cooperation from studies involving Simultaneous as Sequential punishment is significantly lower than cooperation from studies involving based on Sequential leadership-by-example as Sequential punishment.
When comparing studies where Sequential punishment is Simultaneous and studies where Sequential punishment is Sequential turn-taking, cooperation from studies involving Peer as Punishment agent is significantly higher than cooperation from studies involving based on Other as Punishment agent.
When comparing studies where Punishment agent is Peer and studies where Punishment agent is Other, cooperation from studies involving Simultaneous as Sequential punishment is significantly lower than cooperation from studies involving based on Sequential turn-taking as Sequential punishment.
